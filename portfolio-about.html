My Story

I'm a passionate technology enthusiast currently pursuing my BCA degree, with a strong foundation in programming and IT support. My journey began with curiosity about how things work behind the scenes, which led me to dive deep into the world of software development and system administration.

What sets me apart is my commitment to learning by doing. Rather than just theoretical knowledge, I believe in hands-on experience. This philosophy drove me to complete prestigious courses like CS50x, CS50P, and CS50 Cybersecurity from Harvard, as well as earning the Google IT Support Professional Certificate.

I'm particularly drawn to automation and problem-solving through code. Whether it's building a password manager CLI tool, automating web scraping tasks for clients, or setting up virtualized environments with KVM and Ansible, I enjoy creating solutions that make processes more efficient.
